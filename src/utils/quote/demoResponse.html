64-0306

A Greater Than Solomon Is Here Now

God don't need any interpreter.
We interpret, we say, “This, this is that, and this is that,” and so forth.
God don't need an interpreter. He is His Own interpreter. God don't need us to interpret His Word. The Bible, It's written, and It said It's of no private interpretation. God said, in the beginning, “let there be light,” and there was light. That's the interpretation of it. God said, “a virgin shall conceive,” and she did. That's the interpretation of it. It doesn't need anybody to interpret.
God said, in this day, these things would happen, and they are. It don't need any interpretation. It's God doing His Own interpretation. It happens. No matter how much we try to twist It, and say, “It don't mean This, and don't mean That.” It means just exactly, and God is His Own interpreter. He vindicates His Word, and that's His interpretation of It, because It's brought to pass.
